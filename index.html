Welcome to ECE229 New York Airbnb Recommendation’s documentation!¶

This project is a New York Airbnb Price Analysis and Recommendation System.

The housing rental market in New York City is gradually recovering after a rough time during the pandemic. The economy in NYC also revives with the reopening of restaurants, the growth of tourism, and the rapid rising of housing rentals. It is crucial for travelers, long-term tenants, and landlords to understand the housing rental trend in NYC and locate their desired offers, either to stay for a few nights or to optimize the pricing strategy for their own properties. In the website, we provide local hosts and prospective travellers with an interactive and easy to use system that helps them to better understand the rental housing market in New York City. The system is deployed on AWS servers and it includes sophisticated modules for data pipeline, analytical models, results, visualization, and interactive on-the-fly analysis. Please be aware that a large portion of the project was developed in HTML/CSS/Javascrpt, while this documentation only includes the python functions documentations.

Package Dependencies¶

The project depends on flask, numpy, pandas, scikit-learn and bokeh, etc. The packge version is listed below:

For Data Analysis:

->pandas                        1.4.2

For Prediction:

->pandas                        1.4.2
->numpy                         1.22.4

For Test:

->pytest                        5.0.1
